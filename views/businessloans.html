Eligibility Criteria for Business Loan

The eligibility criteria matter a lot to avail of a Business Loans India. There are certain conditions that the business owners or entrepreneurs should satisfy to get a loan from the banks. The criteria are as follows:

  • The age of the candidate should be between 22 to 65 years.
  • The minimum salary of the applicant should be 50,000 INR.
  • Your business turnover from the past two years should be higher than your capital invested.
  • You should have at least three years of experience in the business.
  • Your company should have a regular income of a minimum of Rs. 2 lakhs.
