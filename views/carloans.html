Eligibility Criteria for Car Loans

The eligibility criteria are different for different banks. Here we have shortlisted the requirements that most banks abide by to shortlist an individual to sanction Car Loans.

  • The age of the car loan applicant must be between 21 and 65 years.
  • The minimum monthly salary of the individual should be Rs. 25,000. The self-employed individual's annual income must be no less than Rs.3 lakhs.
  • The individual must have worked with the current employer for at least the past year. The self-employed individual must be in the business for at least two years.
  • Whether salaried or self-employed, business people or individuals working for a government organization or a private company can apply for a car loan.

How to Get Your Car Loan Approved Faster?


Maintain your Credit Score

The credit score is crucial for the loan borrower and the lender. If you seek a car loan at a lower interest rate, you have to ensure that your credit score is 750 and above. If the credit score exceeds 750, the interest rate will be slightly higher. An application with a low credit score is highly prone to rejection.


Pay EMIs On Time

You should pay off EMIs promptly to get in the good books of the loan lender. Make sure to pay your EMIs promptly, as the credit profile will be considered by the lender while approving the loan. If you pay the EMIs on time, your lender will likely grant you the car loan quickly.


Compare Car Loan Interest Rates

You can get a car loan to purchase a new or used car, and there are not one but multiple types of car loans available at different interest rates. Depending on your requirement and repayment flexibility, you can choose the right car loan for yourself.


Go for the High Down Payment Option

It is a good idea to go for a high down payment option as it will reduce the amount of loan taken from the bank. It will further reduce the number of EMIs, the repayment tenure, and the interest rate of Car Loan Finance.


Go with the Budget

Always go for a car loan within your budget as it may affect your repayment capability. Don't go overboard with the loan, as it may be difficult for you to repay it if it is out of your budget. Especially when you have other EMIs to pay, please don't take a loan out of your budget as it may push you to financial troubles.


Choose Loan Option Wisely

Always choose a loan option with low monthly EMIs and short repayment tenures. Don't fall prey to the tricks of the lenders and get into a loan that is too expensive for you to pay.
